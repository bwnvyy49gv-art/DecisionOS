# 🧭 DecisionOS Principles

These principles define how DecisionOS is designed, developed and improved.

Every new feature should support these principles.

If a feature violates one of them, it should be reconsidered.

---

# 1. Humans decide.

DecisionOS never makes decisions for people.

DecisionOS provides knowledge, orientation and transparency.

The final decision always belongs to the user.

---

# 2. Clarity over complexity.

People usually do not need more information.

They need more clarity.

DecisionOS should reduce uncertainty,
not overwhelm users with endless options.

---

# 3. The next step is more important than the perfect plan.

Many people become stuck because they search for the perfect career.

DecisionOS encourages progress through small,
realistic next steps.

Action creates clarity.

---

# 4. There is no universally "best" career.

There is only the career that fits a person's

- values
- interests
- strengths
- goals
- desired lifestyle

DecisionOS never ranks professions by prestige,
salary or status.

---

# 5. Every profession matters.

Society needs

- scientists
- engineers
- artists
- teachers
- nurses
- politicians
- software developers
- craftsmen
- entrepreneurs
- social workers
- designers
- researchers
- farmers

DecisionOS respects every meaningful career path.

---

# 6. Recommendations must be explainable.

DecisionOS should never say

"Trust me."

Every recommendation must answer

Why?

Which information influenced the recommendation?

Which alternatives exist?

What are the advantages and disadvantages?

---

# 7. Privacy comes first.

Personal data belongs to the user.

DecisionOS follows a Local First philosophy.

Cloud synchronization should always be optional.

No personal information should ever be shared without explicit consent.

---

# 8. Growth never ends.

A career is not a single decision.

DecisionOS supports people through

school

training

university

career

career changes

lifelong learning

---

# 9. Emotion matters.

Career decisions are emotional.

DecisionOS does not only analyze skills.

It also acknowledges

uncertainty

fear

motivation

stress

hope

confidence

These emotions are valid and should be considered.

---

# 10. Education is empowerment.

DecisionOS should never simply recommend.

It should teach.

Users should understand

why something fits,

which skills are needed,

how to learn them,

and how to build their future independently.

---

# 11. Technology serves people.

Artificial Intelligence is a tool.

The purpose of DecisionOS is not to replace human thinking.

Its purpose is to improve human decision making.

---

# 12. Build for decades.

Every architectural decision should support

maintainability

transparency

modularity

scalability

Open Source collaboration

DecisionOS should still be understandable many years from now.

---

# Our Promise

DecisionOS does not promise certainty.

DecisionOS promises orientation.

It helps people move from

confusion

to

clarity.

One step at a time.
